Santorini sunset

Can we all just agree on this one little thing?  Santorini is gorgeous.  The white buildings.  The blue sky.  The turquoise ocean.  The cliffs and stairs.  Yeah, it's gorgeous.  Without a doubt, Santorini was our favorite part of Greece.  But, this post isn't about the scenic shots.  I've got some of those coming.  Instead, I wanted to highlight one of my very favorite parts of Santorini: the doors.  Not Jim Morrison Doors but real actual entryways in worn splintered paint of varying shades of blue, green, red, and yellow.
