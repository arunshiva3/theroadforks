Actually, it's not so bad, especially in comparison to London, itself. Most of the gardens had discounted admission or it was around 5 - 10 pounds for both of us. There are lots of fantastic free walks (which are some of the best sights) in the Cotswolds and Cornwall area --- the moors, for example, are completely free! Accommodation can be expensive but definitely cheaper than London. Actually, I'd say the part that was the most expensive was eating out --- pub lunches could run up to around 30 to 40 pounds for a relatively simple lunch. We tended to buy groceries and cook at home and eat out only for lunch.
