in savannah

Details

Bonaventure Cemetery is one of the must-see places in Savannah.  It's outside the central downtown area and absolutely humongous, so wear a good pair of shoes and prepare to spend a couple of hours just walking around.  You'll find lots of photographers throughout the cemetery and a few dogs sniffing around in the grassy banks.
